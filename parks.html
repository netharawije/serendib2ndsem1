Where to Travel

Sri Lanka is a biodiversity hotspot with a rich array of wildlife, making it a paradise for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. The country boasts a network of wildlife sanctuaries that are home to diverse flora and fauna, many of which are endemic to the island. These sanctuaries play a crucial role in conservation efforts and offer visitors a unique opportunity to witness the natural beauty and ecological diversity of Sri Lanka.
